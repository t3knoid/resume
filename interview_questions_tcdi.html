TCDI Interview Questions

Sure! Here are some interview questions based on your experience as a Technical Applications Engineer at Technology Concepts & Design Inc:

  1. Automation and Scripting:

    • Can you describe a specific instance where you automated network inventory management using Ansible scripts? What challenges did you face, and how did you overcome them?
    • How do you ensure that your Ansible scripts for security patches and updates are reliable and secure?

  2. API and SQL Development:

    • Can you provide an example of a playbook you developed using APIs and SQL? What was the purpose, and what were the key outcomes?
    • How do you approach integrating APIs with SQL databases to ensure seamless data flow and functionality?

  3. Asset Management:

    • How did you manage key assets like GitHub Enterprise, TeamCity, JFrog Artifactory, JFrog XRay, and SonarQube? Can you discuss any specific improvements or optimizations you implemented?
    • What strategies did you use to maintain and monitor these tools to ensure they were always up-to-date and functioning correctly?

  4. Virtual Machines and Server Support:

    • What are some common issues you encountered while supporting virtual machines on Windows and Ubuntu Linux, and how did you resolve them?
    • Can you explain your role in maintaining Nuix servers for e-Discovery? What specific tasks were involved?

  5. Software Development:

    • Can you discuss a project where you developed software in C# and Java? How did you integrate Nuix and Relativity APIs into your solutions?
    • What best practices do you follow when developing software to ensure code quality and maintainability?

  6. General Technical Skills:

    • How do you stay updated with the latest technologies and tools relevant to your role as a Technical Applications Engineer?
    • Can you describe a particularly challenging technical problem you solved during your tenure, and what steps you took to address it?
